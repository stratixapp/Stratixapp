Our Story

About Stratix

A software ecosystem built from a single belief: Indian businesses deserve world-class tools at prices that make sense.

Leadership
Founded With Purpose
AS

Ananthu Shaji

Founder & CEO · Lyceum Corporation

Ananthu Shaji founded Stratix with a clear mission: to build premium software products that genuinely serve Indian businesses, professionals, and creators — without the complexity or cost of enterprise tools designed for Western markets.

Operating under Lyceum Corporation, Stratix has grown from a single idea into a full ecosystem of 12 products spanning legal tech, GST/fintech, business management, spiritual wellness, and creative tools. Every product is built mobile-first, integrated with India's payment and compliance infrastructure, and designed to work as well offline as it does online.

The vision: a unified, trusted software brand that Indian entrepreneurs and professionals choose first.
