FAQ
Common Questions
How do I get started with Stratix? ▼
Visit our Products page, pick any app, and start for free. No credit card required for free tier. Upgrade when you need more.
Are your apps available on Play Store? ▼
Yes — Stratix Pro, GSTLens, MyRight, and Grimoire are all available on the Play Store via TWA. Search "Stratix" on Google Play.
Do you offer a free trial? ▼
Every Stratix product has a generous free tier. You can use core features for free indefinitely and upgrade to Pro for advanced capabilities.
Is my data safe? ▼
Absolutely. We use Firebase with strict Firestore security rules, encrypted authentication, and never sell user data to third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I pay? What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets via Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway.
Can I request a custom feature? ▼
Yes! Send us your request via WhatsApp or email. We prioritize features that serve the most users, and enterprise clients can request custom builds.
